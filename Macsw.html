Key: Where to Get or Use Software
Sites Campus Computing Sites have some software packages that you can use while you're there.
Sales You can buy shrink-wrapped copies of some software complete with manuals at the U-M Computer Showcase
Download Some software can be downloaded free of charge by the U-M community (note: you may be required to enter your uniqname and UMICH password)
Licensing You can purchase licenses to download and use some software. (Note: manuals do not come with these licenses)
ITD Login Service This service lets you use certain software packages from any computer that is connected to the campus network.
ITD Statistics and
Computation Service 		This service lets you use certain computing-intensive software packages (mostly scientific and programming) from any computer that is connected to the campus network.
