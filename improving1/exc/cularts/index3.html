Welcome
a letter from the dean. It talks about the school, the programs that are available, our faculty, and the students that attend the Madonna Lousie Ciccone School of Culinary Arts. The dean also discusses our recent news, including the awarding of our annual Flambe St. Jacqueline Head Croupier Medallion, which has gone to one of our students, a junior.

Programs
undergraduate and graduate degree programs to suit your interests. Programs can be customized as well, so as to suit those who are not able to attend the nrmal classday schedule, or those who want a personalized style of education. For more information, see the Programs page.

Faculty
hailing from a variety of backgrounds, our faculty represent a wide range of epicurean tastes. Perhaps our best-known faculty member is Dom Deluise, who likes barbecue dishes and desserts shaped like things.

Administration
staff and services that keep us in hollandaise sauce! Actually, these staff are the same people who work in our head office at 2244 Mason Hall, from 8am to 5pm Monday through Friday. They offer a variety of support services, ranging from the normal to tasks which many people do not do.
