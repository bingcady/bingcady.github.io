Art

My favorite art came from the 30s and 40s in America, from the Regionalist, Social Realist, and Magic Realist schools. Some of these works portayed a fantastic depth of feeling.
  • Peter Blume (especially The Rock and The Eternal City)
  • Grant Wood's lesser-known works (like Death on the Ridge Road)
  • Edward Hopper (post-1926)
  • an O. Louis Guglielmi painting the River
