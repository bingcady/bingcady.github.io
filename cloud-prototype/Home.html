Cloud Computing @ U-M

The university wants to invest IT resources in the activities that support the work it does best: teaching, learning, knowledge, and clinical care.

 

Instead of purchasing expensive equipment or software, the university can use services available over the Internet, services known as cloud-based services.

 

This approach to IT delivery means you can focus on advancing your mission, not managing your technology.

 

This website introduces Cloud Computing @ U-M, from basic definitions of what it means to compute in the cloud and what services are available today to how to connect with others using cloud services on campus.

 

More resources on how to get started with cloud offerings on campus and beyond will be added in coming months.
