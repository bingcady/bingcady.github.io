My grandfather was involved in the inception of the television industry. And my dad's been in computers since '68. Though I was intrigued by both of those fields and what they promised, I began my career in library work and print publishing.

Netscape 1.0 changed that. When I saw that interface, I knew I'd found the link between all these worlds; the next great medium, the new frontier in communication. I started my home page within a month, and I haven't stopped since.
