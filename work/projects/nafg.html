This project was my first (aside from my home page), created in 1996 for the National Adjunct Faculty Guild.

Critical to the site's success was fast download time, as many of the Guild's members were not provided at-school Internet access and so had to dial in from home. Also important to the Guild's director was that the site exude a bright, friendly image; this was reflected in the front page colors and in the chalkboard and pencils theme.

The site has been very successful in spreading the word about the Guild and in recruiting new members. At last count, there were over 400 sites linking to this one.
