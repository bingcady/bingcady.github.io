The main site to get you going in the right direction will probably be that AudioWeb list of audio-related sites. It includes a link to the World of Audio site and the Soundwave site, which look like they might have some stuff. The Audio FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) list on the AudioWeb list might have some good info too. It's just a bunch of text documents, but the FAQs are usually well-informed.
